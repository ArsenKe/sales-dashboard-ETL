# Cleaning Company Dashboard - Client Manual

## Overview
This dashboard provides real-time insights into your cleaning business operations, including:
- Job scheduling and tracking
- Employee performance metrics
- Customer management
- Revenue analysis
- Service optimization

## Accessing the Dashboard
- **URL**: http://46.225.78.208:3000 (or your domain)
- **Default login**: admin@example.com / password

## Key Features

### 1. Job Management
- View all scheduled and completed jobs
- Track job status in real-time
- Assign jobs to employees
- Record actual hours worked

### 2. Customer Insights
- Customer lifetime value
- Service frequency analysis
- Contract management
- Payment status

### 3. Employee Performance
- Hours worked per employee
- Quality ratings
- Utilization rates
- Compensation tracking

### 4. Financial Dashboard
- Daily/Weekly/Monthly revenue
- Outstanding payments
- Profitability by service
- Cost analysis
